VidaWell

Landing Page

I designed a landing page for VidaWell, an extension of Dasana. This page serves as a comprehensive presentation of VidaWell, where I highlight relevant information, including benefits, available plans, contact details, and collaborations with prominent brands. VidaWell focuses on enabling businesses to develop their own applications based on Dasana to strengthen their customer relationships outside of their establishments and enhance the well-being and quality of life of their employees.

Electium

App Mobile

I designed a mobile application for Electium with the aim of providing users with the ability to maintain a personal virtual diary. This app allowed users to write, upload audio and photos, and establish two trusted contacts during the registration process. Years later, these contacts would receive the content stored in the diary. The app also offered other important features. Users could explore blogs from various content creators. Additionally, the app allowed users to track payments associated with their funeral services.

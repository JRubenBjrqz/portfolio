Enhancing Online Presence
with a Landing Page Update

Client TopSpin Music
Timeline 6 Weeks
Tools Figma
Role UX/UI Designer

Overview

TopSpin Music is a Los Angeles-based company specializing in providing a wide range of services, including digital advertising, data analysis for the music industry, creative services, and digital marketing. Their clients range from artists and managers to distributors and record labels. Currently, the company is in the process of updating its landing page with the goal of attracting new clients. Additionally, they have future plans to develop a comprehensive dashboard that will integrate all their services in one place, thus providing a more efficient and convenient experience for their users.

Research

Conducted a competitive analysis with the aim of identifying benchmarks, assessing key differences, and exploring opportunities that could inform the landing page redesign process. Furthermore, I conducted interviews with users to delve deeper into their current experience with the site, gathering valuable insights to enhance usability and user satisfaction.

Findings:

  • Recommended the implementation of an integrated website form instead of relying on external services like Google Forms. This would simplify the user interaction flow and increase visitor retention.
  • Suggested including a language toggle button that allows users to switch between English and Spanish intuitively.
  • Proposed creating a section that highlights previous collaborations with well-known brands and companies. This would strengthen credibility and attract the attention of potential clients.
  • Advocated for reducing the amount of text on the website and exploring ways to distribute it more effectively. This would make information easier for visitors to digest and keep their attention.
  • Proposed a review of content structuring for a more intuitive and seamless navigation, enabling users to find relevant information more efficiently.
  • Recommended the inclusion of animations and videos to enrich the user experience and provide visually appealing and dynamic content.

Defining the Solution

For the redesign of the landing page, I identified the following key recommendations:
  • Recommended the strategic incorporation of animations on the website. However, it's crucial to avoid animation overload to maintain a clean and user-friendly design.
  • Proposed avoiding information overload on the website. The key is to segment and present information directly and concisely so that users can clearly understand what is being offered.
  • Suggested designing the website to be responsive and adaptable to various screen sizes, as users will access it from different devices.
  • Advised implementing language options on the site, with an emphasis on English as the primary language, given its relevance in the market.
  • Recommended including a form or registration process to enhance site credibility since redirecting users to a form can instill greater trust and commitment.

Wireframing

For the wireframing process in this project, I first conducted a competitive analysis and gathered references. Then, I sketched out an initial draft by hand, and finally, I created grayscale wireframes for client evaluation. This approach allowed for precise design and effective feedback.

Establishing the Visual Design

Transformed a website that lacked personality into a modern and appealing platform. I used bold colors, including the popular dark mode, to breathe life into it. I also added a language toggle button for international users and implemented a user retention system to measure site performance. These improvements significantly transformed the user experience.

High Fidelity Prototype

I had the opportunity to collaborate closely with the client. The conversations and tests conducted were crucial in understanding the specific project needs and effectively adjusting the design. Some of the key changes agreed upon during this phase included:
  • Segmenting information into different pages to enhance navigation.
  • Graphical improvement of buttons, providing a more attractive and user-friendly appearance.
  • Modernization of the overall design style to give it a contemporary and fresh look.
  • Implementation of a custom form on the page to ensure the security of user data.

Conclusions and Learnings

This project has been an extremely enriching experience for me. Conducting a competitive analysis and learning from similar companies has been crucial and has significantly contributed to the product's improvement. One of the primary objectives was to optimize both the flow and design of the website, and I believe that segmenting the information, including language options, and implementing the success stories section are successful elements that strengthen TopSpin Music's image.

Collaborating with industry experts has been an invaluable educational experience. The ability to understand their needs and translate them into a project that reflects their vision has validated my skills in meeting their requirements and delivering innovative design. It's important to note that this project is still in discussion and negotiation with the client, and everything presented in this case study has served as a fundamental basis for the final delivery.
